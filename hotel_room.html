Different Rooms available at our hotel along with their description

Single Room


Embrace the elegance, comfort and luxury that await you in our Single Rooms. This room is assigned to accommodate one person and has a single bed. It also includes a dresser, cable television, a writing desk, and a sitting chair. It evokes a style with modern amenities that todays discerning traveler demands.

Double Room


Our Double Rooms have classic elements, sophisticated modern design and d√©cor. This is a room with one double bed and has maximum occupancy of two people. It provides extra space in the bedroom area featuring full length armoires, ample work space, an intimate sitting area, while some rooms have large windows supplying copious natural light.

Triple Room


Tastefully decorated with classically patterned carpet and sophisticated furnishing with a distinguished look yet warm feel. Whether you are traveling for business or pleasure these rooms boast lefroy brooks bathroom fixtures with oversized bath towels, large spacious vanity area, upgraded Molton Brown amenities and double-wide shower with rainfall showerhead. This room is assigned for three people. It has two beds. The third bed may be a fold out bed.

Quad Room


Ideal for travelers in town for more than a couple of days, spacious Deluxe Queen rooms offer an intimate sitting area. All Deluxe Quad Room bathrooms boast oversized bath towels, a double-wide shower stall including our famous rainfall showerhead, and a vanity mirror. This room is assigned to four people. It is most suitable for accommodating two adults and two children.

Family Room


Relax in a spacious, luxuriously appointed guest room, featuring two double-size beds with fluffy duvets and Frette bed linens. Deluxe Family Rooms also offer a full-length closet, two sitting chairs and a coffee table ? perfect for families or guests who need ample space for sharing. It accommodates two adults along with two or more children.

King Room


Featuring an enhanced king-size bed, our Deluxe King rooms are exclusively designed for the discerning traveler. Bathrooms are comprised of double-wide showers with rainfall showerheads. All Deluxe King rooms boast an intimate sitting area, oversized bath towels and a vanity mirror; a larger 35-inch LCD HDTV flat-screen television completes the picture. This room is occupied by a single person.

Mini-Suite


Mini-Suite that we offer, is one of the most luxurious hotel rooms. Also called as a Junior Suite, it is a single room with a single bed and a sitting area. These suites are particularly marketed to cater business needs. We have frequent business travelers that appreciate the additional space and use it to host small meetings and entertain their clients.

Suite


Our suite denotes a class of luxury accommodation, also called as the presidential suite. It offers multiple rooms, with more space and furniture than any other standard room. Dining, office and kitchen facilities are also available. In addition to one or more beds and bedroom fixtures, our suite includes a living room with a couch that converts into a bed.
